For each question you can lean toward an answer (one click), commit with conviction (two clicks), mark yourself agnostic, hold that there’s no fact of the matter, or skip. A few questions let you pick a combination of answers. At the end you’ll see how your views compare with the survey’s spread of professional philosophers. Your answers are saved only in this browser. Question wording follows the published survey design (Bourget & Chalmers, Philosophers on Philosophy: The 2020 PhilPapers Survey).
